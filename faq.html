TecKnow is a source for what's new and popular on the web and the technology world. Users can view and provide all of the content and decide, what's good and what's junk. Links that receive community approval will see them featured on the home blog page, so the front page is constantly in motion and filled with fresh, interesting links.

What’s is a blog?

Let’s begin with some definitions. A bit dry, we realize, but this is a necessary evil. First we’ll define the word this whole site is based around – blog. A blog is a frequently updated online personal journal or diary. It is a place to express yourself to the world. A place to share thoughts and your passions. Really, it’s anything you want it to be. For our case a blog is for educating people website that we are going to update on an ongoing basis. Blog is a short form for the word weblog and the two words are used interchangeably.
